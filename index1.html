My Career

Gaming on PC Since 2009

22/05/2009 I recieved my first PC game - PvZ, from my father. I played through the entire game within the year.

12/08/2010 I started playing my first MMO - World of Tanks, on the very first day of its release.

03/02/2011 I got my 3rd game - Age of Empires III. This one took me a little longer: I played through the hardest difficulty (w/o cheats) after 2 years.

24/12/2013 This day saw the transition of me from playing strategy games to action games as I received Battlefield IV for Xmas.

2015 I shifts my main focus onto WoT as it matures and played my 10,000th match.

2016 I hit 60% win ratio in WoT and becomes a Unicum (top 5%).

2017 I tried out Battle Royale games but quickly lost interest.

2018 I played my 100,000th game in WoT but disappointed at the P2W situation. (declined to be a community contributor)

2019 I started revisiting the Battlefield series

Looking Forward

My future career

I invested over 15000 hours on World of Tanks, a game which I fell in love with. Over the past few years, I have been invited by many WoT eSport teams in Asia, as well as various WoT tournaments. However, given the status that I am sill a student, I declined all of these offers. Thus, I hope that I can get to participate in one of the tournaments in the future - when I am old enough to commit myself.

As of now, I have no intention of becoming a professional eSports player and would like to keep PC gaming as my hobby.
